## 🛠️ **Troubleshooting**

### **Camera Not Working?**
1. **Test camera first**: `python3 simple_camera_test.py`
2. **Check permissions**: System Preferences → Security & Privacy → Camera
3. **Use upload option**: Works perfectly as fallback
4. **Restart terminal**: Sometimes needed for permissions

### **App Not Loading?**
1. **Check URL**: [http://localhost:8501](http://localhost:8501)
2. **Restart app**: `streamlit run app.py`
3. **Check terminal**: Look for error messages

### **AI Not Working?**
1. **Check model file**: `ASL_Model.pth` should be present
2. **Check dependencies**: All packages installed
3. **Try upload option**: Often more reliable

## 🎯 **Pro Tips**

1. **Camera positioning**: Good lighting, clear background
2. **Hand placement**: Center your hand in the camera view
3. **Multiple attempts**: Try different angles for best results
4. **Use upload option**: Often more reliable than camera
5. **Practice regularly**: The more you use it, the better it gets
